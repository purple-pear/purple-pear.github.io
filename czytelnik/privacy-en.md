# Privacy Policy - Czytelnik App

Your privacy is important to us. Below is information about how the application processes data:

- **No user account:** The application does not require you to create an account or provide personal data (such as name, surname, or e-mail).
- **Advertisements:** The application uses the Google AdMob service to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data, such as device advertising identifiers, for personalizing ads and analyzing their effectiveness.
- **Local data:** The application stores downloaded books, reading history, and settings (e.g., font size) only in your device's memory.
- **Network connection:** The application connects to Wolne Lektury servers (wolnelektury.pl) to download the book catalog and text and sound files.
- **Analytics:** The application may collect anonymous statistical data regarding errors (crash reports) to improve operational stability.
